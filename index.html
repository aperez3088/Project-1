Marsha P Johnson

Marsha P Johnson (August 24 ,1945-July 6,1992) The P in Marsha P Johnson stood for "Pay it no mind". She had established the Street Transvestite Action Revolutionaries which stood for (STAR) in 1970 in New York. Marsha was tragically murdered at 46.Her case was closed by NYPD but later on was opened by a transgender activist by transgender activist Mariah Lopez reopened for investigation in 2012. In her early years she became a drag queen in New York. The police men were targetting this LGBTQ protests and calling them "riots", "rebellion", and uprising. Her body was found in the Hudson River off the West Village Piers. An important historic figure in Black History Month and in general because of her perseverings and the many people she helped.
